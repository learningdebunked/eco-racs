PROJECT OVERVIEW
----------------
Complete, production-ready implementation of the "Carbon-Aware Checkout" 
research paper by Kapil Poreddy. A novel AI system for real-time basket-level 
emissions scoring in e-commerce.

NOVEL METRICS IMPLEMENTED
--------------------------
✓ COG (Carbon Opportunity Gap) - Maximum achievable reduction
✓ BAE (Behavior-Adjusted Emissions) - Realistic expected reduction
✓ RACS (Risk-Adjusted Carbon Score) - Uncertainty quantification
✓ MAC_basket (Marginal Abatement Cost) - Economic analysis
✓ RPE (Recurring Purchase Emissions) - Annualized impact
✓ CHCS (Composite Carbon-Health Score) - Joint optimization
